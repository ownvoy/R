__combine vectors in a row or column__
```r
c1<-c(2,4,6,8,10)
c2<-cbind(x, c1)
c2
```
```r
     x c1
[1,] 1  2
[2,] 3  4
[3,] 5  6
[4,] 7  8
[5,] 9 10
```

```r
c3<-rbind(x,c1)
c3
```

```r
  [,1] [,2] [,3] [,4] [,5]
x     1    3    5    7    9
c1    2    4    6    8   10
```

__create matrix__
<br>

__two row matrix with 1 to 10__
```r
m1<-matrix(1:10, nrow=2)
m1
```
```r
     [,1] [,2] [,3] [,4] [,5]
[1,]    1    3    5    7    9
[2,]    2    4    6    8   10
```
<br>

__three columns matrix with 1:6__ 
```r
m2<-matrix(1:6, ncol=3)
m2
```
```r
     [,1] [,2] [,3]
[1,]    1    3    5
[2,]    2    4    6
```
<br>

__matrix filled by rows, defalut: filled by cloumns__
```r
m3<-matrix(1:6, nrow=2, byrow=T)
m3
```
```r
     [,1] [,2] [,3]
[1,]    1    2    3
[2,]    4    5    6
```
